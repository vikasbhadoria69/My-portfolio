Out with the old...

Life, study, work

Studying just to pass the exams is easy. Committing your time, following through with that original concept and applying the learned concept to solve a real world problem, that's the tricky part.

I first started working as a German counsellor but later to pursue my higher education in Germany I decided to merge my startup with an Indian company. This allowed me to focus more on my studies as we were able to hire people to off load my work pressure.

Why did I decide to go for higher education? I've been asked this question a lot of times as to why I decided to go when I was doing well with my startup. Well, I have done my bachelor's in electronics and always had passion for technology. I wanted to explore opportunities and there was a lot to learn. Coming to Germany was one of the best decisions I took. I was able to combine my theoretical knowledge with practical implementation which helped me learn a lot and stay close to the technical field.

How did I end up having interest in artificial intelligence? Being from an electronics background I never had subjects related to AI or machine learning. I was always interested in network security. Once I read an article on how to create an 'intrusion detection system' using machine learning. That was the time when I realized the power of machine & deep learning. I started to read a lot and took online courses which helped me develop skills related to these technologies. I really enjoy working with these current technologies. The future really excites me!!

The current project that I am working on is my master's thesis, I am trying to build a self driving car prototype using computer vision & deep learning techniques. The idea is to build a car that can use a camera sensor to follow lanes, detect road signs/signals and react accordingly. I choose this topic because the idea of fully automated cars really fascinates me.
